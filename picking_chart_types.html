What is Data Visualization

Data visualization is the graphical representation of information and data. ’

The use of visual elements like graphs to provide an accessible way to see and understand patterns in data.

Additionally, it provides an excellent way to present data to non-technical audiences,

Exploratory Charts vs Explanatory Charts

The Dataset

  • The Palmer Penguins Dataset

  • Collection of morphological measurements for three penguin species observed in the Palmer Archipelago, Antarctica.

  • It was introduced by Dr. Kristen Gorman and the Palmer Station Long Term Ecological Research (LTER) program.

  • It is a high-quality, popular dataset for data science and visualization.

  • Out of Comfort Zone - Truely Exploratory

Key Variables

  • Type of Species (Categorical)
  • Name of Island (Categorical / Spatial)
  • Sex (Categorical)
  • Bill Length (Continuous Variable)
  • Bill Depth (Continuous Variable)
  • Body Mass Index (Continuous Variable)
  • Year (Time)
